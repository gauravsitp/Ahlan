A Complete List of Oscars 2020 Winners

14 feb 2020

The film revolves around alien invaders who kidnap the planets superheroes geared up to join on the action

Hot on the heels of the news that Angelina Jolie and Natalie Portman will star as the next female superheros, Priyanka Chopra is geared up to join in on the action. The former BAZAAR cover star will star in a new Netflix film called We Can Be Heroes, written and directed by Robert Rodriguez.

Whilst there is no word yet on the role Priyanka will be playing, the Quantico actress will be seen alongside the likes of Pedro Pascal, YaYa Gosselin and Akira Akbar in the film.

We Can Be Heroes circles around a group of alien invaders who kidnap Earth's superheroes, leaving children to fend for themselves and save their parents along with the world, according to Hollywood Reporter.

Priyanka's last project was Netflix production Isnt It Romantic, in which she starred alongside Rebel Wilson and Liam Hemsworth.
